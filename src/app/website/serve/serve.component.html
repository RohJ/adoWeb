Join us in serving the Kingdom of God by being a part of a team where you can fellowship and grow together with others who share the same passion and heart as you.

Serving Team

The Serving Team focuses on ensuring that every person who walks into the Church feels at home. The team displays hospitality and they go out of their way to help people in the Church. They are discipled to exemplify Godly character and moral integrity in their acts of service to the people. If you enjoy meeting new people and working behind the scenes, this is the place for you to help as well as grow to be Christ-like.

Worship Team

The Worship Team serves the Church by ensuring that there is an atmosphere that helps people experience the Glory and Presence of God. To see the full release of His Kingdom on earth as is in heaven through worship. It is led by talented and committed musicians that serve on Sundays, as well as during Worship Nights, Hours of Prayers, Conferences etc.

Outreach Team

The Light Bearers is the youth outreach Team that carries the heart of the Great Commission that was given to the disciples in the Gospels. The team focuses on outreaches to take the Gospel to the people outside the church. This is done while looking at discipling all the members in the team so each person may grow in intimacy with God and boldness to preach His Word.

Media Team

The Media Team ensures that people can experience the Presence of God in the Church and also in their homes. The cameras, lights, screens, and sounds are all operated by the team. The team also has social media coordinators, graphic designers, content writers, web designers and more. They ensure that they support the Gospel being presented on the stage, and take it online to reach others.
