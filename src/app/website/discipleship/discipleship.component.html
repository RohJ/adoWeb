Starting Strong in Your Discipleship Journey

While discipleship should be straightforward and accessible, many face challenges that can make it intimidating. Trying to navigate this journey alone can be time-consuming and frustrating, especially with the overwhelming amount of information available. Without clear guidance, this often leads to uncertainty about where to begin and a feeling of leaving your spiritual journey up to chance.

If you find yourself wondering how to get started, our church is dedicated to offering the guidance and resources you need to confidently pursue your discipleship journey.

Why Discipleship?

  • Transference of ability

Keys to Victorious Living

"Keys to Victorious Living" is a 12-week course designed to help Christians mature in their faith, inspired by Jesus' method of teaching His disciples, it emphasizes personal transformation and the transfer of abilities. The goal is to empower participants to live victoriously and equip them to disciple others using the truths they learn throughout the course.

Each group will consist of 5 to 10 participants, and a host will be assigned to facilitate interaction and discussion.

Topics

  • The Belt of Truth
  • The Breastplate of Righteousness
  • The Gospel of Peace
  • The Shield of Faith
  • The Helmet of Salvation
  • The Sword of the Spirit
  • The Breastplate of Righteousness
  • Devotional Life

Building a Godly Foundation

"Building a Godly Foundation" is a 17-week course designed to help Christians lay a strong spiritual foundation in their lives, essential for fulfilling the Church's mission.

As a sequel to 'Keys to Victorious Living,' this course emphasizes the importance of understanding one's purpose in life and living with an eternal perspective, relevant to both leaders and the congregation.

To encourage interaction and discussion, participants will be placed in groups of 5 to 10, with each group assigned a host.

Topics

  • A New Beginning
