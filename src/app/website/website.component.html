Our Conferences

The Encounter with God is a weekend programme that enables people to live life in abundance and experience freedom from rejection, curses and other areas they are struggling with. The teachings help establish a Godly foundation and answer various questions that people have about God. Through these sessions, we have witnessed many people healed and restored to wholeness through the understanding that comes from experiencing The love of God. The programme caters to people across various denominations and geographic locations."

All of us undergo disappointment, hurt, and trauma; and if not dealt with, can often be held captive to them for life. Having been set free from the spirit of rejection and experienced freedom, Rev. Victor D’Monte set forth on a journey to discover the ministry of deliverance.

At Adonai Ministries, it is our desire to see people restored from brokenness to wholeness and be equipped in the ministry of healing and deliverance. This ministry imparts a message of hope to the hurting by providing the stepping stones to train others on how to effectively minister to those in need.The Wholeness and Freedom Seminar provides it’s delegates with the skills as well as practical application in which they can grow in discernment.

Driven by his burden and passion for supporting pastors and leaders, he saw the need to organize a conference for pastors and full-time workers to be encouraged. Through the Global Pastoral Conference, Rev. Victor D'Monte endeavors to unite pastors and leaders from various backgrounds, cultures, and experiences, creating a space to learn, grow, and find encouragement as they serve the Body of Christ.
