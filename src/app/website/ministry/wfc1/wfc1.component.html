Restoring Lives and Equipping People

All of us undergo disappointment, hurt, and trauma; and if not dealt with, can often be held captive to them for life. Having been set free from the spirit of rejection and experienced freedom, Rev. Victor D’Monte set forth on a journey to discover the ministry of deliverance.

The Wholeness and Freedom Conference birthed in 2009, was founded on the Ministry of Jesus and seeks: “…to preach good tidings, to heal the broken-hearted, to proclaim liberty to the captives, and open the prison to those who are bound, to comfort all who mourn; to give them beauty for ashes, the oil of joy for mourning, the garment of praise for the spirit of heaviness…”

Through the teaching of the Word, key principles of inner healing, deliverance, and discipleship are imparted to the delegates. The teachings are based on extensive biblical research, training, and ministry experience.

Each delegate is given the opportunity to receive a one-to-one personal ministry by a well-trained Ministry Team member. We ensure that the highest value of confidentiality is maintained with those who minister to the delegates.

This 7-day programme aids in bringing healing & wholeness to lives that have been damaged by disorders & diseases in spirit, soul, and body.
