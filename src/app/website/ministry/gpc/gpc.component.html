Elevate Your Impact In Ministry:
"Ignite a Healthier Church Community in the midst of a morally deteriorating world."

This is a non-denominational forum dedicated to serving & nurturing the Body of Christ by:

Creating a Collaborative Platform:

We aim to be a community which fosters collaboration between diverse segments of the Body of Christ, working towards building a healthy global Church.

Providing Practical Pastoral Training:

We are committed to working with pastors and leaders, providing them with practical training to address the unique challenges of the 21st Century Church.

Establishing a Safe Haven for Ministers:

Our conference offers a safe, nurturing environment where pastors and leaders can receive ministry in an un-prejudiced atmosphere.

Confidentiality is an integral value upheld by the ministry team.

Empowering Leaders:

We seek to empower leaders by equipping them with teachings and resources in the inner healing and deliverance ministry.

At the Global Pastors Conference, we endeavor to strengthen Pastors & Leaders worldwide, foster unity, and equip leaders to effectively serve their communities, making a lasting impact on the world.

BUILDING A CHURCH MINISTRY TEAM

As a pastor, trying to meet the emotional and spiritual needs of people within the church, can be overwhelming. But having a well-trained ministry team will provide a safe place for those who struggle in their spiritual life due to unresolved hurts and memories of the past.

As a ministry, we have many years of experience in developing ministry teams and also providing resources that could help churches fulfil their pastoral role.
