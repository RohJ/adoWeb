"He comforts us in all our troubles so that we can comfort others..."
2 Corinthians 1:4

At Adonai Ministries, it is our desire to see people restored from brokenness to wholeness and be equipped in the ministry of healing and deliverance. This ministry imparts a message of hope to the hurting by providing the stepping stones to train others on how to effectively minister to those in need.

A significant part of Adonai Ministries is to be a resource to the local Church for the strengthening of their leadership. The Wholeness and Freedom Seminar provides it’s delegates with the skills as well as practical application in which they can grow in discernment.

On completing this seminar, the delegates have the opportunity to join the Adonai Ministry Team and a first-hand experience in ministering to people.

ATTENDING THE WHOLENESS & FREEDOM PART-1 IS A PRE-REQUISITE TO ENROLL FOR THE SEMINAR.

*PERSONAL MINISTRY AND CONFIDENTIALITY

The delegates have the opportunity to receive personal ministry towards the end of the programme. We ensure that the highest value of confidentiality is maintained with those who are ministering to the delegates.
