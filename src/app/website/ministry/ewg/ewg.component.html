“They Devoted Themselves To The Apostles Teaching And To Fellowship, To The Breaking Of Bread And To Prayer.” Acts 2:42

The Encounter with God is a weekend programme that enables people to live life in abundance and experience freedom from rejection, curses and other areas they are struggling with. The teachings help establish a Godly foundation and answer various questions that people have about God.Through these programmes, we have witnessed many people healed and restored to wholeness through the understanding that comes from experiencing The love of God. The programme caters to people across various denominations and geographic locations.

Attending all sessions is necessary to avail personal ministry.

*PERSONAL MINISTRY AND CONFIDENTIALITY

The delegates have the opportunity to receive personal ministry towards the end of the programme. We ensure that the highest value of confidentiality is maintained with those who are ministering to the delegates.
