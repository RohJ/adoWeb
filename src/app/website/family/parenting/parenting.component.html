Parenting Course

The Parenting Course is a series of five sessions designed by “The Alpha Course” to help parents train up their children in the way they should go, so when they are old, they won’t depart from it. The formative years (0-10 yrs) when children are developing their worldviews is most critical. As parents, we must recognize the weight of the responsibility entrusted to us by God and raise them in an environment that values faith, instilling a strong sense of identity and purpose in them.

Topics

• Build a strong foundation
• Meet our children's needs
• Nurture their gifts and talents
• Set boundaries
• Teach them how to cultivate healthy relationships
