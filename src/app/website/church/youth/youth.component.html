Ignite the Spirit Within

At Youth Alive, we aim to create a vibrant and inclusive community where young people can encounter the love of God, grow in their relationship with Jesus Christ, and be equipped to live out their faith boldly. Through dynamic worship and engaging teaching, we aim to empower young people to live out their faith in every area of their lives and build authentic lasting relationships.

Vision

  • To provide a place of belonging, where love and acceptance abound.
  • To ignite a passion for Christ within the hearts of our youth.
  • To teach and equip them with the Word of God, building a strong foundation of faith.
  • To create an opportunity for them to discover and function in their spiritual gifts, building up the body of Christ and serving the needy.
  • To reach out to the city and extend Christ's love to our community.
