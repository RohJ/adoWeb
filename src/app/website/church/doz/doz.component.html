Daughters of Zion

“And pray in the Spirit on all occasions with all kinds of prayers and requests. With this in mind, be alert and always keep on praying for all the Lord's people.” Ephesians 6:18.
The Daughters of Zion are women that come together weekly to pray for the Church. They intercede for the congregation and the different ministries of the church. They are also the prayer warriors for our nation and its leaders.

They meet in person every first Tuesday of the month, and online on the remaining Tuesdays of the month.
