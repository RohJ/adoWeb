Teens

"Don't let anyone look down on you because you are young, but set an example for the believers in speech, in conduct, in love, in faith and in purity." 1 Timothy 4:12

EMPOWERING THIS GENERATION TO WIN THE NEXT

The Teens Ministry caters to the young people in Church (aged 15 to 19). They have regular meetings twice a month where they learn and grow in the Word as well as understand & express themselves to God and other people.

PARENTAL INVOLVEMENT

Parents & caregivers are invited to be a part of their teen's journey with Christ. Beginning from Changes, and through the interactive and fun activities, parents are consistently involved to assist them in grooming their teen spiritually & otherwise.

DISCIPLESHIP

A personal “cushion” is always around for the teens & pre-teens to fall back on. They are provided with a safe environment to explore, experiment, discuss, and develop their God-given gifts with personal disciplers alongside them. The teens are also encouraged to carry down what they have learnt to younger teens.
