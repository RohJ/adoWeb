CURRICULUM

Kingdom Kids follows Group India's Faithweavers curriculum for its children. All the children are taught similar topics on different levels in accordance with their age groups. The teachings are split into parts with different activities as well. This is so that all the different types of learners in the group are able to grasp the truth that is being taught. As there is a focus on kinesthetic learning, the children are able to understand and retain all that is taught to them in class. In 40 lessons the course aims at covering the entire Bible in a year.

There are also various different activities that take place throughout the year like Fun Sunday, Prayer Sunday, VBS, Outreach and a Christmas Programme. Through all of this, there is a focus on holistic teaching and practical application that goes beyond what is just spoken of in class. Children are encouraged to grow in the Word and hear God in all that they do, wherever they are.
