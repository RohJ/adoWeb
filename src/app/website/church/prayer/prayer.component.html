Prayer & Fellowship

“This is the confidence we have in approaching God: that if we ask anything according to his will, he hears us”.1 John 5:14God says, "My House shall be called a House of Prayer." We come together twice a month to be refreshed in the Presence of God and call upon His name in prayer and intercession. Through these 'Prayer Nights' we have seek God's Kingdom and His will to be established. One of the best ways to improve in prayer is to do it in community.

They meet every fourth Saturday from 10:00am to 1:00pm.
